About us

At Empire Construction, we believe every project should stand the test of time. Since our founding in 2005, we've completed over 1,200 projects ranging from residential remodels to large-scale commercial developments, with a total project value exceeding $500 million. Our average project completion time is under budget and ahead of schedule by 10%, a testament to our meticulous planning and hands-on management.

Founded by James Harrington and Maria Lopez, two lifelong engineers with a shared passion for quality and innovation, Empire Construction began as a small family-run business with just five employees. Today, our team has grown to over 150 dedicated professionals who uphold the same values of integrity, craftsmanship, and client-first service that our founders instilled from day one.

We partner closely with clients at every stage, ensuring that projects are not only delivered efficiently but also exceed expectations in durability, design, and sustainability. With a combined over 20 years of industry experience, our leadership team has overseen projects ranging from multi-million-dollar corporate offices to bespoke residential estates, always emphasizing safety, precision, and long-term value.
